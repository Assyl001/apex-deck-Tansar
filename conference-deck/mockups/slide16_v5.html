Case Study · From Our September Paper

Sovereign Wealth Fund Manager Oversight.

The Challenge
75 external managers
Quarterly review cycles, manual data, late warnings.
Quarterly reviews too slow for fast-moving risk.
Insufficient early warning before exposures move.
Manual data compilation delayed strategic decisions.
The Implementation
L3 monitoring system
Daily ingestion across all 75 managers.
Performance, holdings, risk metrics — synced daily.
Anomaly detection across strategies and sectors.
Unified dashboard — one view for the whole portfolio.
Then — one Tuesday — the dashboard surfaced something.
