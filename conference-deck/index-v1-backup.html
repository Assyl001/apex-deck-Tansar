The L0–L5 Framework — six levels of autonomous investing

Level Name Behavior Human Role
L0 No Automation All analysis manual. AI tools only support. Full control
L1 Investor Assistance Single-task automation. Data collection. Alerts. Human-in-the-loop
L2 Partial Automation Integrated analysis. Sentiment + valuation. AI synthesizes. Human supervises
L3 Conditional Automation AI recommends buy/sell within defined domain. Human-on-the-loop
L4 High Automation Full autonomy within defined operational domain. Human-out-of-the-loop
L5 Full Autonomy Across all market conditions. Theoretical. Policy-only

L3 is where the institutional industry can credibly be today.
